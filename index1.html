Core findings & observations

01

The primary driver for the 58.3% untagged rate was the missing Pain Point tag in CRs 3, 4, 5, & 6 (corrected on 10 March 2026).

02

CR 1 and CR 5 represent 65% of all tickets that used invitation-related CRs.

03

CMs sometimes manually add the Pain Point tag to tickets, with the majority of these manual tags applied by Stephanie H.

04

An anomaly exists (likely a bug) where Groove occasionally deletes the Pain Point tag automatically after auto-application.
